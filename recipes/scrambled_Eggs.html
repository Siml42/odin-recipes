Scrambled Eggs

Description

The right way to scramble eggs. There is more to just mixing eggs and cooking! This will make a believer out of you.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons cream
  • 1 pinch salt and pepper to taste

    • Steps

    1. In a cup or small bowl, whisk together the eggs and cream using a fork.
    2. Melt margarine in a skillet over low heat. Pour in the eggs, and stir constantly as they cook. Remove the eggs to a plate when they are set, but still moist. Do not over cook.
    3. Add salt and pepper
